How to Play

Object: Find all three treasures buried at Kaboom Beach! The treasures are randomly positioned in an 8x8 grid, and they may be arranged horizontally or vertically. You will have a limited number of turns to complete this task, and danger is lurking below...

Gameplay: Dig a hole in the sand by selecting a square. If part of a treasure is there, you will see a mysterious golden glow shining from the ground. Once you completely uncover a treasure, it is considered "found", and placed next to the dig site. Your progress and the number of remaining turns are indicated in the status bar below the grid. Find all three treasures to win the game.

Your chosen difficulty determines the number of turns you have to complete the game.

  • Easy...............30 turns
  • Medium........25 turns
  • Hard.............20 turns

    • Select the "Activate Mines" switch before starting the game, and 4 mines will be randomly placed within the dig site. Selecting a square will now reveal a number representing the number of mines buried in the adjacent squares. Should you be unlucky enough to hit a mine...KABOOM!! The game is over.
